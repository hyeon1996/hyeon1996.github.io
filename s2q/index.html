Retaining Suboptimal Actions to Follow Shifting Optima in Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

Yonghyeon Jo, Sunwoo Lee, Seungyul Han
UNIST / MLIC
Accepted to ICLR 2026
Code Cite

Abstract

We study the challenge of shifting optima in cooperative multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL), where agents prematurely discard suboptimal actions that later become necessary for coordination. To address this issue, we propose S2Q, a method that explicitly preserves useful suboptimal behaviors to improve adaptation and robustness under changing coordination dynamics.
